why people use CoinControl

Budgeting:
Coin Control helps user set budgets for various expenses, such as rent, groceries, and entertainment, and keep track of their spending within those categories.

Debt management:
Coin Control helps users too keep track of their debt payments and monitor their progress towards paying off loans or credit card balances.

Saving:
By tracking their expenses, users can identify areas where they can cut back and save money. Coin Control also helps users to set savings goals and track their progress towards them.
